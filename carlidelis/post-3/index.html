"The Social Dilemma" Got Us Talking About the Ways Social Media Affects Our Mental Health

Trigger warning: this article discusses suicide.

By Marie Gooley & Caitlin Paroczai

Caitlin Paroczai, Writer at Student Edge: Absolutely everyone on the planet has been talking about Netflix’s latest documentary The Social Dilemma recently, so I decided to toss any fears I had aside and watch it.

Let’s just say: there’s a lot to unpack here. The doco explores how big social media companies manipulate users using algorithms, how they harvest personal data to target users with ads, and how fake news can impact our actual behaviours. So my workmate Marie and I had a chat about it.

The whole doco makes me want to rock back and forth in a fetal position. However, the part that shocked me the most is how social media is affecting our mental health. What did you think, Marie?
